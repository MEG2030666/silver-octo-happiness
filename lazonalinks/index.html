About LaZona Links


LaZona Links is a small business based out of Phoenix, Arizona. Our designers collaborate to create quality websites that suit your business needs. Don't need a website just yet? We can also help with your Graphic Design needs. Our designers have experience in creating flyers, logos, graphics, presentations, etc.



Meet the designer


Megan LaRose is the founding designer of LaZona Links. She is a member of the Navajo Nation and a full-time student at Maricopa Community Colleges (MCC). Megan continues to expand her design portfolio as she continues her education at MCC. She is a great communicator and values giving back to Indigenous communities.
